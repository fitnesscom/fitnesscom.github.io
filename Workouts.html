LAWNMOWER PULL

Primary muscles: Back Secondary muscles: Core, lats, shoulders, legs Equipment: Dumbbells


Stand straight with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a dumbbell in your left hand. Squat as you rotate your torso to the right and bring the dumbbell toward your right foot. Stand up, rotate your torso slightly to the left and raise the dumbbell until it's close to your left shoulder. Switch sides and repeat.
